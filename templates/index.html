Why Improv Helps Your Spanish

Traditional learning uses focused thinking (grammar, rules, drills). Improv activates Diffuse Thinking—a relaxed state where your brain makes new connections faster. By focusing on the scene and your partner, you stop translating in your head and start reacting in the moment.
